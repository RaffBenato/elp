Protection Block

On arrival at the protection block the engineer's train should stop and wait for permission to proceed.
After receiving permission from the possession master to allow the engineers train into possession, the protection block marker person will lift the protection and show the driver a yellow flag / hand lamp and inform them that they may proceed to the next block, at caution speed.
Note: If a PNX signal is being used for the protection, be prepared to carry out the rule (Rule Book 5)

To enter the block ETO will receive a yellow hand signal / lamp.
